Key Metrics Explanation

Accuracy

Percentage of correct predictions out of total predictions.

Precision

Percentage of predicted threats that are actually threats.

Recall

Percentage of actual threats that were correctly identified.

Specificity

Percentage of normal traffic correctly identified as normal.

F1-Score

Harmonic mean of precision and recall.

ROC-AUC

Area under the Receiver Operating Characteristic curve.

True Positives (TP)

Threats correctly identified as threats.

True Negatives (TN)

Normal traffic correctly identified as normal.

False Positives (FP)

Normal traffic incorrectly flagged as threats.

False Negatives (FN)

Threats that were missed by the model.
